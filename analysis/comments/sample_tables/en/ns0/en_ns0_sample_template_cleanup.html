'''Avalanche Skills Training''' is a standardized form of [[avalanche]] training in [[Canada]]. The training is provided in two levels: AST1 and AST2. AST 1 includes basic snow study and training on the use of a [[avalanche transceiver|transceiver]], [[Avalanche#Probes|probe]] and [[Avalanche#Shovels|shovel]] in companion rescue. AST2 concentrates on advanced snow study. Further training instruct the use of explosives for [[avalanche control]]. AST is supported by the [[Canadian Avalanche Association]] and the [[Alpine Club of Canada]].
